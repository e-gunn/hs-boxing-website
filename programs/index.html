Programs

Amateur Boxing Program

Members from ages seven to 18 participate in a weekday afternoon boxing training program designed to improve mental toughness, physical fitness, attention, discipline, and focus, while at the same time providing a safe space to occupy their time. A combination of participation rate, improvement in school grades, and commitment to the program. A successful member will graduate high school and continue on to college, enroll in the armed services, or secure full-time employment.

Amateur Boxing Events

The organization produces local, regional, and state amateur boxing events and tournamants for its members to participate in. Creating opportunities for the members to compete and showcase their skills keeps motivation levels high to stay in the program. All programs serve the at-risk youth of Miami-Dade County, Florida.
